# RoutePilot AI – Project Overview

## Vision

RoutePilot AI is Dell's next-generation autonomous logistics decision engine — a unified enterprise platform that transforms reactive, manual supply chain operations into an intelligent, self-optimising ecosystem powered by AI, machine learning, and 3D digital twin technology.

---

## Problem Statement

Dell's global logistics network faces five critical challenges that cost the business millions annually:

| Challenge | Current Pain | Business Impact |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **No unified visibility** | 12 hubs, 8 TPRs, 178 part types — no single pane of glass | Decisions made on stale reports |
| **Static routing** | Fixed carrier rules ignore real-time congestion, SLA risk | 18% preventable transit delays |
| **Cost overruns** | No What-If simulation, no multi-lever optimisation | 23% logistics cost premium |
| **Reverse waste** | 31% of returned parts unnecessarily scrapped | $1.2M recoverable asset value lost |
| **No SLA intelligence** | Teams react to breaches after they happen | SLA penalties + customer dissatisfaction |
| **Linear supply chain** | No circular economy lifecycle management | 2,847 tonnes CO₂e emitted annually |

1. **Eliminate route inefficiency** — score every route across 14 AI parameters in real time

## Key Innovations

### 1. 14-Parameter AI Route Scoring
Every route is evaluated across 14 weighted parameters — distance, cost, SLA risk, hub capacity, carrier reliability, transit time, historical performance, weather risk, priority level, carrier type, congestion index, load factor, hub availability, and part criticality — producing a composite confidence score.

### 2. 8-Stage Circular Economy Lifecycle
The first logistics platform to implement a full circular economy engine: Collection → Triage → Refurbishment → Redeployment → Component Harvesting → Material Recovery → Responsible Recycling → Carbon Accounting.

### 3. 3D WebGL Digital Twin
An immersive Three.js WebGL command center renders all 12 hubs as animated 3D nodes with real corridor flow particles and live AI alert overlays — giving executives spatial situational awareness impossible in 2D dashboards.

### 4. ML SLA Breach Prediction
Random Forest Ensemble trained on 1,800 historical transactions. 7-vector risk scoring per shipment. 12-month forecasting capability. SHAP explainability for every prediction.

### 5. Judge-Ready Demo Mode
A dedicated presentation engine with auto-guided 8-step tour, story overlays, keyboard shortcuts, fullscreen mode, and live/demo data toggle — built to showcase every capability in under 5 minutes.
